About The Blog

Traversing from Himalayas in north all the way to Indian ocean in the south, India is a land of many marvels. It offers many wonders to be explored making it a country worthy of being in every travellers bucket list. So get on a virtual ride with us to relish the beauty of this wonderful country - INDIA!!.

India has been a tourist friendly country since ages. Being the ideal host is in the genes, India is moving ahead in the Tourism Sector with its scenic forest beauty, picturesque mountains, vast beaches and famous pilgrimages. The country is committed to entertain and provide with excellent facilities and hospitality to welcome the Travelers from all across the world. The government has envisaged to establish India as an attractive tourist spot with a view to develop its heritage and tourist spots. Indian state of Gujarat is known as having the only natural habitat of Asiatic Lions. It has various kinds of ecosystem, ranging from marine biodiversity, inland wetlands, saline deserts to tropical deciduous forest. Various iconic sites like "Taj Mahal" and glorious past have further boosted the tourist flow into the country. Tourism in India has grown manifold during last two decades with its tourist friendly policy and esthetic approach.
